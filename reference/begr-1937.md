## Why it's in `reference/` not `01-foundations/`

This pamphlet argues the **philosophy** (verb-elimination, linguistic fictions, language as a machine).
The **operational rules** a learner needs are distilled into `../01-foundations/grammar-rules.md`.
Read this for the *why*; read foundations for the *how*.

## Key takeaways for distillation

- "850 words will say clearly and simply almost everything we normally say with 15,000–20,000."
- Output should be **indistinguishable from Standard English** for the reader.
- Core innovation = "a new approach to the functions of the Indo-European verb" ? the operator system.
- "Translation and Reduction" + "Verb-Elimination" sections are the best models for a **reducer** prompt.
