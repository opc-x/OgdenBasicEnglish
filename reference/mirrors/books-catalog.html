    Ogden hoped to have 1,000 works of great writers of the present and past and 1,000 books of general knowledge in the sciences as a seed for Basic English. He was well on the way when he died, as this list shows.

    Books about Basic English  or  English as an International Language:

  • Basic English compiled by Julia E. Johnson. Wilson, Reference Self Series, Vol 15 No.1, 1944. A collection of 30 essays on Basic English to satisfy the curiosity generated by Winston Churchill's encouragement before the Congress of the United States in 1943. Ten descriptive by Ogden, Richards, etc., 10 favorable, and 10 critical . - Good.
  • Basic English for Adult Education by the Department of Adult Education of the National Education Association to give the whole setting of Basic English in its larger aspects, to give some picture of what it is over and above its usefulness to foreign-born Americans.
  • Basic English and Its Uses, Richards, I.A., 1943, Norton & Co.,New York. Includes a list of books on or in Basic. (Many books, from the Bible to H.G. Wells' stories, were translated to Basic, to popularize it and prove that it was adequate to saying anything.)
  • Basic in Teaching: East and West, Richards, I.A., 1935, Psyche Miniatures. China experience.
  • Basic English versus the Artificial Languages, C.K. Ogden
  • C.K. Ogden : A Collective Memoir, edited by P. Sargant Florence and J.R.L. Anderson ; Clarke, Doble, & Brendon Ltd, 1977. ISBN: 0301 76061 6. 252 pages.
  • Brighter Basic, C.K. Ogden 1931. Examples of Basic
  • C. K. Ogden: a bio-bibliographic study by W. Terrence Gordon. Scarecrow Press, Metuchen, N.J. & London, 1990 . Summery of Ogden's life (55 pp) and complete bibliography (96 pp) .
  • Counter-Offensive : An Exposure of Certain Misrepresentations of Basic English Introduction by W.     Not Basic English - avoid confusion

  • Basic International Language Quick International Understanding in One-Tenth the Time Using International Words by Wesley E Arnold, June 1993. This is Esperanto. Trade paperback, 104 pages, ISBN: 0915935120
  • Basic English for Science by Peter Donovan, Aug 1978, Oxford Univ Press, 149 pg. ISBN: 0194571815
    This is introductory standard English for non-native speakers preparing to study a science subject. $11.50.
  • English Picture Word Book by Hayward Cirker and Barbara Steadman. This is a coloring book with objects named. Page one introduces apple, bird, and cat, but also paper plate, clothes pole, and picnic table. Although not Basic English, it is an educational toy that supports learning language.
