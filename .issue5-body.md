## 方法论核心：不是翻译卡片，是排列组合

Ogden 的底层逻辑不是"把 4000 个动词翻译成 BE850"，而是：

```
18 operator × 10 方向 × 850 名词池 = 覆盖日常英语 90%
```

训练就是**反复操练这套排列组合**，直到不假思索。

---

## 三步走训练结构

```
Step 1: 18 Operator 基本功 (~90句)
  → Step 2: Op × Direction 排列组合 (~350句)
    → Step 3: Op组合 + 名词 → 场景句 (~300句)
```

### Step 1: 18 个 Operator 基本功

| Operator | 练什么 | 例句 |
|----------|--------|------|
| put | 放置 | I put the book on the table. |
| take | 拿取 | I take the book off the table. |
| go | 移动 | I go to the station. |
| come | 朝向 | Come to the table. |
| get | 获得 | I get the book from the table. |
| give | 给予 | Give the book to me. |
| make | 制造 | I make a cake. |
| keep | 保持 | Keep the book on the table. |
| let | 允许 | Let me go. |
| seem | 似乎 | The book seems good. |
| be | 存在 | I am in the room. |
| do | 做 | I do my work. |
| have | 有 | I have a book. |
| say | 说 | I say yes. |
| see | 看见 | I see the train. |
| send | 发送 | Send the letter to me. |
| may | 可能 | I may go. |
| will | 将要 | I will go. |

### Step 2: Operator × 方向 = 排列组合

**这是 Ogden 的发动机。** 每个 operator 配 6-10 个方向，每个组合 2-3 句。

| 组合 | 替代动词 | 例句 |
|------|----------|------|
| put + on | wear | Put the book on the table. Put your coat on. |
| put + off | postpone | Put off the meeting. |
| put + out | extinguish | Put out the light. |
| take + off | remove | Take your coat off. |
| take + out | extract | Take the book out of the box. |
| go + in | enter | Go in through the door. |
| go + on | continue | Go on with your work. |
| come + in | enter | Come in through the door. |
| get + up | rise | Get up from the bed. |
| get + on | board | Get on the train. |

~90 个组合 × 3-5 句 = ~350 句

### Step 3: 排列组合 + 850 名词 → 场景句

| 场景 | 例句 | 用到的组合 |
|------|------|-----------|
| My Room | My bed is against the wall under the window. | be + against/under |
| At a Meal | I take my spoon and put the soup in my mouth. | take + put in |
| At the Station | I go to my office in a train. I get a ticket. | go + get |
| The Body | I put my right hand on my head. | put on |
| The Weather | I get an umbrella and put it over my head. | get + put over |
| The Family | I get some milk for my baby brother. | get for |

## 能不能覆盖 90% 日常英语？

1. phrasal-verbs.md 原文：**16 operator × 20 方向 ≈ 替代约 4000 个英语动词**
2. Ogden 1937 原文：**850 词能清楚简单地说出平时用 1.5-2 万词说的大部分内容**
3. 实际验证：put on = wear, put off = postpone, take off = remove, go on = continue, get up = rise... ——日常高频动词几乎全被 operator 组合覆盖
