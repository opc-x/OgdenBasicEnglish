## Ogden 排列组合训练数据 v4 — 806 句（模型直接生成，非模板拼凑）

### 方法论：三步走

```
Step 1: 18 operator × 20 方向 = 75 个有效短语动词
    → 替代 4000+ 普通英语动词
    → 每个短语动词造 6-18 句变体

Step 2: operator + 抽象名词 = 30 个搭配
    → 替代抽象动词（decide → make a decision）

Step 3: 7 个 Ogden 场景 = 70 句连续段落
    → 只用 850 词拼出真实对话
```

### 📊 Step 3: 场景句（70 句，7 场景）

**My Room:**
> This is my room. The door is in the wall. My bed is against the wall under the window. I put my books on the table between the bed and the door. A picture is on the wall over the books. I take the cover off my bed and put it on a seat. The window is wide and the room is bright. I keep my key on the table by the door.

**At a Meal:**
> Some food is on the table. I take my spoon in my hand and put the soup in my mouth. I put salt on my meat and take butter on my bread. The taste of sugar is sweet. This cake is good. I take the skin off the orange. Come to the table. The meal is ready.

**At the Station:**
> I go to my office in a train. I get a ticket at the station. I give a ticket to my friend and put the other in my pocket. Here is my train. The front is not full. The porter gives the bag to me. I get off the train at my station.

**The Body:**
> This is my body. I have a head, two arms, and two legs. I put my right hand on my head. I take my right hand off my head. My mouth is between my nose and my chin. I have a tongue in my mouth. This finger is bent. That finger is straight.

**The Weather:**
> The weather is warm in the summer. In the winter the weather is cold. Rain is wet. It is water from the sky. In wet weather I get an umbrella and put it over my head. The sky is clear today. The sun is bright.

**The Family:**
> My father is a man and my mother is a woman. I have one sister and one brother. My brother is very young. He is still a baby. I get some milk for my baby brother. A kiss and a smile make a baby ready for play.

**Going to the Store:**
> I go to the store on the street. I get some bread and butter at the store. I put the bread in my bag and go back to my house. I take money from my pocket and give it to the man. After the store I go to the office in a train. At the end of the day I get on the train and go home.

### 组合爆炸公式

```
75 个 op×方向 短语动词
  × 平均 8 个名词/句变体
  × 5 种时态变换（现在/过去/将来/否定/疑问）
  = ~3000 句 Step 1 可扩展量

30 个 op+名词 搭配
  × 10 句变体
  = ~300 句 Step 2

15 个场景 × 20 句
  = ~300 句 Step 3

总计: ~3600 句核心 + 自由组合可到 10000+
```
