Read material written in (or near) the 850-word set. Comprehensible input is the practice core.

## Exercises

1. **Picture description** — describe a photo using only 850 words. Log words you reached for but couldn't use; find the BE850 build.
2. **Reduction** — take a normal-English paragraph, rewrite in BE850 (replace verbs with operators, drop fancy words). The 1937 pamphlet's "Translation and Reduction" section models this.
3. **Operator drill** — pick a standard verb, build it from operator+direction without looking.

## Output to distill folder

Save your reductions and error log; they become training/eval pairs in `../05-distill/`.
